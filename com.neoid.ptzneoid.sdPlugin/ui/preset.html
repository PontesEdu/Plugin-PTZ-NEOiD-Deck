PTZ NEOiD - Preset

A preset is a saved camera scene that lets you quickly return to a specific position and settings. You can save up to 10 presets (0–10). To call a preset, simply press the button; to save one, hold the button for 1 second (long press). There’s also an image box option: if selected, the preset saves a snapshot and shows it on the button. Each image is linked to the camera’s IP, so different cameras can have their own preset previews.
